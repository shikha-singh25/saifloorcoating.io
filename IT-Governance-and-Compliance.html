With more than 10 years of combined experience in government contracting, our practice leaders help clients address complex regulatory compliance, audit and other government oversight burdens.

    Clients rate us 9.8 out of 10 when asked to answer one question on a 1-10 scale. “Will you refer Sofsecure to a friend?” We believe our client's satisfaction and our people's satisfaction are related, as reflected in our “Best Place to Work” awards.
